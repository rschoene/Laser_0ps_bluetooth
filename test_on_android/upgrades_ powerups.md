# Power-Ups

## General:

Have to be unlocked with XP

# Power-Shot

Next shot hits hard

# Super shot

Hit all enemies with next shot

# Radar

Show all players location (for multiplayer)

# Upgrades

## General

Each has 3 levels

## Health

Adds more health points to blaster


## Munition

Adds munition per reload

## Damage

More damage per shot

## Reactivation time

reduces time to reset after kill

## Reload Time

Reduces Reload time
